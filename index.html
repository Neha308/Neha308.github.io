Vote Coach is a website that aims to help people who may not know much about their local elections and encourage them to exercise their right to vote. We also want to make voting easier by presenting them with a survey that will narrow down the candidates in certain elections. In addition to helping them decide, we give them an overview of polling stations that are nearby. Remember, our main goal is to help you VOTE for what floats YOUR boat!
